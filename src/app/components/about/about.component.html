Who is Ognen Stefanovski?

Ognen Stefanovski is a Macedonian Athlete specializing in the Decathlon and currently holds seven national records for his country. Ognen is known for his record-breaking abilities and for rewriting the track & field record books in Macedonia.

He has achieved multiple "national champion" titles in the U18, U20, and Senior divisions, and has represented his country in numerous international championships, including the European and Balkan championships. Ognen has been representing Macedonia in the national dress since 2022.

He is proudly competing for his club, AK Delta, which has been the best club in Macedonia for 5 years. Ognen has improved the national records in the Decathlon and Heptathlon a total of eight times since 2024. His achievements continue to impact Macedonian athletics and encourage the younger generations of athletes.
